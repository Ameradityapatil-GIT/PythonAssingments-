Google is a multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products.
Its flagship product is the search engine which dominates the online search market. The company was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, and is headquartered in Mountain View, California.
Apart from search, Google also offers a range of services such as Google Ads, Google Drive (cloud storage), Chrome (web browser), Google Maps, 
and YouTube (video sharing), among others.
